Global Sales Analysis Dashboard
Business OverviewThis project features an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard designed to track global commercial performance across multiple dimensions including geography, product categories, and time. By synthesizing raw transactional data into visual insights, this tool enables stakeholders to identify high-growth markets and optimize inventory management.

Key FeaturesPerformance Metrics (KPIs): Real-time tracking of Total Sales ($79.68k), Total Quantity (707 units), Top City (Los Angeles), and Top Product (Laptop).Geospatial Intelligence: An interactive map visualizing sales distribution across North America, Europe, and Asia (Uzbekistan).Product Performance: A bar chart ranking products by volume, showing Tablets as the highest-volume item followed by Monitors and Printers.Regional Analysis: Breakdown of revenue by country, highlighting Canada ($8.6k) and Spain ($6.4k) as leading markets.Year-over-Year Growth: A waterfall chart depicting sales trends from 2024 to 2025, highlighting periods of significant "Increase" (Uvelichenie).

Data ArchitectureThe analysis is built upon a structured dataset (see image_134420.png) featuring:Geographic Data: City, Country, State/Province, and GPS Coordinates (Latitude/Longitude).Product Details: Categorization by item type (e.g., Tablet, Phone, Printer, Monitor).Sales Transactions: Tracking of Date, Quantity sold, and Revenue generated per entry.Regional Grouping: Classification into North, South, West, East, and Central regions.
